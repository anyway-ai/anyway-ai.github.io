FAQs.

Reach out to us, describe your use-case in as much detail as possible, and let us handle the rest. Our team will keep you in the loop with intermittent samples, and depending on how complex your use-case is, we'll deliver the dataset to you in record time. Decompress it and start training. Simple.

We spent a year building a network of AI models whose sole purpose is to generate datasets that can train downstream AI models. They're able to understand the information you provide us with about your use-case and autonomously generate a synthetic image dataset. Think "a mapping between your natural language to scalable datasets".

Similar to our process of synthetic image generation, annotations are also generated completely autonomously. What's more, they're generated in parallel with each image, which means there's no overhead in labelling your images.

Every dataset that Anyway.ai generates comes with a validation report. Just before delivery, our team performs exhaustive testing on the generated dataset; we even take into account the quantitative validation metrics that matter to you. Before you train, you should have a good idea as to how the dataset will perform in production.

Apart from a simple service fee, you'll incur a variable per-image cost. This is entirely dependent on the complexity of your use-case and annotation requirements. Get in touch with us to know more.
